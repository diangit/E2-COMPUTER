Hi there! I'm Dian.

Mil Adrian Nares Gile, popularly referred to as "Dian," is an ambitious and driven young adult. Dian is a 20-year-old student studying Bachelor of Science in Criminology at Sorsogon College of Criminology Incorporated, wherein he manifests eagerness in terms of the lessons regarding the crime and justice system while inculcating interest to serve in his community as a passionate uniformed personnel member of the Philippines. Dian has had a fascination for criminology since childhood, due to experiences of heroism and service from family members and local heroes. He has always admired the discipline and sense of duty as law enforcement workers. More inspiration was gained with the improvement witnessed in the safety and justice system due to committed officers. Now, Dian is driven not only by the want to serve but also by the want to help build a community where people will be protected and respected as well. Outside of his books, Dian is an athletic enthusiast who would occasionally throw himself into basketball.
